## üìã Feature Overview

The Maintenance Module includes 6 major features:

### 1. üìÖ Calendar View
Visual monthly calendar showing all maintenance activities with color-coded priorities.

### 2. üîß Preventive Scheduler
Create and manage recurring maintenance schedules with automatic alerts.

### 3. ‚úì Task Checklist
Complete task management with status tracking and user assignment.

### 4. üì¶ Spare Parts Management
Inventory tracking with low stock alerts and location management.

### 5. üí∞ Cost Tracking
Record and analyze maintenance costs by type and task.

### 6. üìú Maintenance History
Complete historical records with filtering and detailed cost breakdown.
